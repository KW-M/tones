TONES

Welcome to Tones, a geometric music maker that uses different techniques of waveform generation and sampling to allow you, the player, to create many different sound scapes. Try placing different shapes around the canvas & experiment. Keys 0-5 to change shape. Scroll wheel to change size.

Details

The different geometric shapes to represent different waveforms. For example, circles produce sine waves, triangles play triangle waves, squares are square waves, etc. The placement of shapes modifies their harmonics and the size changes their volume.
